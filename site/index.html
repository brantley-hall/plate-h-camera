Research Tool  ·  Washington Square, New York  ·  c. 1839–1895

Plate H

A geometric and photographic analysis of the original NYU Gothic Revival building and its early documentation

Working research site. This tool reconstructs the optical geometry of early photographs and engravings of the NYU University Building (1835–1895) at Washington Square East. Camera positions, sight lines, and plate projections are computed against a georeferenced 1852 plan.
